Connor Dietrich

Hi! My name is Connor Dietrich and I am a senior (Class of 2025) at Fossil Ridge High School and a concurrently enrolled student at Front Range Community College. I have a whole range of interests. Everything from tennis, trains, computers, cars, remote controlled electronics, electrical engineering, all the way to the beauty of nature and photography!

During my time at Fossil, I've done many things. I am an active, and founding member of the Computer Science Honor Society at FRHS. I am the last board member who hasnt graduated yet. I've also worked my way up to team captain of the FRHS Boys JV Tennis Team and plan on being a team manager for the FRHS Girls Tennis Team during their Spring Season. I've completed the whole STEM program here at Fossil and have both mentored, and been mentored, by my peers. I've made some of my closest friends during my time here and I want to attribute that to the wonderful people who have come into my life through programs such as tennis and CSHS.

TRAINS

Model & Real

With both of my grandfathers being model train enthusiasts, it was hard not to be. From a very, very young age, I have always been fascinated by trains of all size. Every scale of model trains, all the way up to the real deals. I've visited train museums and exhibits

I currently have a 4x4ft & 4x8ft model train boards. They are both running HO (1:87) Scale.My 4x8ft board is my primary board with my 4x4 board being a seasonal Christmas board that gets put up around our family Christmas tree.

My passion for cars ignited early on, fueled by the sleek designs and the raw power they embody. Everything from the classic muscle car to the modern luxary SUV.  More than just modes of transportation, I view them as works of art, each with its own unique character and story to tell. MY CAR

2024 Chevrolet Trax Activ (Right)

My 2024 Chevrolet Trax Activ is my passport to adventure. Decked out with every available package except for the sunroof and wireless charger, it's ready for anything I throw its way.
