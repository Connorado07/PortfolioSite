TRAINS

Model & Real

With both of my grandfathers being model train enthusiasts, it was hard not to be. From a very, very young age, I have always been fascinated by trains of all size. Every scale of model trains, all the way up to the real deals. I've visited train museums and exhibits

I currently have a 4x4ft & 4x8ft model train boards. They are both running HO (1:87) Scale.My 4x8ft board is my primary board with my 4x4 board being a seasonal Christmas board that gets put up around our family Christmas tree.

CARS

HTML, CSS, JS, WordPress

My passion for cars ignited early on, fueled by the sleek designs and the raw power they embody. Everything from the classic muscle car to the modern luxary SUV.  More than just modes of transportation, I view them as works of art, each with its own unique character and story to tell. From classic muscle cars to cutting-edge electric vehicles, I'm fascinated by the engineering and technological innovation behind them. Whether I'm behind the wheel, attending car shows, or simply admiring a beautifully crafted machine, my love for cars continues to grow with every passing year.

MY CAR

2024 Chevrolet Trax Activ (Right)

My 2024 Chevrolet Trax Activ is my passport to adventure. Decked out with every available package except for the sunroof and wireless charger, it's ready for anything I throw its way. The black grille and bowtie emblems, along with those striking 18-inch black aluminum wheels, give it an attitude that matches my own. Inside, the heated seats and advanced infotainment system keep me comfortable and connected wherever I go. This Trax isn't just about getting from point A to point B; it's about embracing the journey and exploring the world in style and comfort.
